ob Title: Data Analyst

Job Summary: We are looking for a detail-oriented and analytical Data Analyst to join our team. In this role, you will work with large datasets to extract, clean, and analyze data, providing actionable insights to guide decision-making across various departments.

Key Responsibilities:

Collect, clean, and organize data from various sources (e.g., databases, spreadsheets, and APIs).

Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies.

Develop and maintain dashboards, reports, and visualizations to communicate findings to stakeholders.

Collaborate with teams (marketing, finance, operations, etc.) to understand their data needs and provide data-driven recommendations.

Create detailed reports that summarize findings and offer actionable insights.

Perform statistical analysis and build models to forecast trends and predict outcomes.

Ensure data accuracy, integrity, and consistency across systems and sources.

Support data-driven decision-making through data visualization and storytelling.

Assist in automating reports and data pipelines for efficiency.

Skills and Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g., Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Economics).

Proficiency in data analysis tools and software (e.g., Excel, SQL, Python, R).

Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).

Strong knowledge of statistical analysis techniques and methods.

Ability to work with large datasets and perform data wrangling.

Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Strong communication skills, with the ability to present complex data in an understandable way to non-technical stakeholders.

Knowledge of machine learning and predictive modeling is a plus.

Experience in industry-specific tools or platforms may be required (e.g., Google Analytics for marketing data analysis).

Preferred Experience:

Experience working with cloud-based data storage (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Azure).

Familiarity with data automation tools and programming languages (e.g., Python, R, SQL).

Knowledge of business intelligence tools and techniques.
